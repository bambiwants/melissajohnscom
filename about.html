About Melissa Johns

Melissa Johns is a multimedia visual artist and graphic designer working on her undergraduate degree for Fine Arts & Business at the University of Waterloo.

Melissa's visual practice focuses largely on mixed media work with a wide variety of traditional and digital media. With a background in self-taught and formal art education, she is able to produce creative solutions which highlight successful experimentation and a high degree of technical ability.

Melissa is interested in exploring themes of intimacy, uncertainty, and mysticism.
