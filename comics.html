 FLUX 

FLUX (2014, mixed media) is a 10 page sequential art piece that explores anxiety and ambivalence as they relate to self-concept.

Final Level (2014, digital) is a comic strip about geek culture, which was published biweekly in Imprint Publications. Art by Melissa Johns, co-written by Matt Lawes and Melissa Johns.

 No Experience Required 

No Experience Required (2014, mixed media) is a comic strip about internships, which was published weekly in Imprint Publications. Art by Melissa Johns, co-written by Matt Lawes and Melissa Johns.
