 Latest Work 

NOTHING TO SEE HERE (2015, lasercut acrylic) is a text-based sculptural series which aims to address the problematic nature of the archetypal suburban lifestyle. A set of multiples, this collection of cookie-cutter houses explores the social phenomena of artificial communities through vapid, benign clich√©s commonly exchanged by neighbours. NOTHING TO SEE HERE dissects social pretense in a playful way; visually distilling white collar values while offering reference to the old adage condemning hypocrisy in the judgement of others.
